
 Type Casting
 ============
 
 Coverts a value from one Datatype to Another Datatype.

 

 Types
 =====

 Widening
 ========
   
          * It converts a lower datatype into Higher Datatype. (When we assign the lower level DT to Higher level DT)
          * It is called widening or Implicit.
          * Compiler will convert the automatically

   Syntax
   ======
          Datatype Varname = Varname;

 Narrowing
 =========

          * It  converts a Higher datatype to lower datatype. (When we assign the Higher level DT to Lower level DT)
          * It is called Narrowing or Explicit.
          * Manualy.
 
  Syntax
  ======

        Datatype Varname = (Datatype)Varname; 



byte - short - int - long - float - double

double d = 100.04; long l = (long)d; int i = (int)l;
